How should we sync Notion → AI?

3 approaches. I recommend A — here's why.

A

Live fetch + cache (Recommended)

On every AI call (coaching, call summary), we call the Notion API, extract plain text from all 3 product sections, and cache it for 30 min. No extra storage needed — Notion is always the source of truth. Supervisor can force-refresh anytime.

Always fresh No DB changes Simple
B

Manual sync → stored in DB

Supervisor clicks "Sync Now" in settings. We pull the Notion page, extract text, and save it to a knowledge_cache table in Supabase. AI always reads from DB. Supervisor sees "last synced: 2 hrs ago" and the extracted preview.

Full control Preview what AI sees Needs DB table Can go stale
C

Hybrid: auto-sync every X hours

Like B but with auto-refresh on a cron schedule (e.g. every 6h). Needs a Supabase Edge Function or cron job. Overkill for a page that's manually edited a few times a week.

Most complex Needs cron setup
My recommendation → A
Your Notion page is already small (3 products, ~3KB of text). A fresh fetch adds ~200ms per AI call and avoids any DB migration. The 30-min cache means it won't hammer Notion. When you update a product script in Notion, the next AI call picks it up automatically.
